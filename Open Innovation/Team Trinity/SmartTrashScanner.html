How It Works

SmartTrashScanner is an innovative solution that helps you manage household waste efficiently. Simply follow these steps:

  1. Take a picture of your trash using your smartphone or camera.
  2. Upload the image to our website.
  3. Let SmartTrashScanner identify and categorize the items for you.
  4. Receive suggestions for responsible disposal and locate nearby dustbins.

Benefits

By using SmartTrashScanner, you contribute to:

  • Reducing environmental impact
  • Promoting recycling and responsible waste disposal
  • Locating convenient disposal options
